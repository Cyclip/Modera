Filters

Toxicity

A rude, disrespectful, or unreasonable comment that is likely to make people leave a discussion.

This filter is very sensitive and may flag harmless messages. It's advised to use the abusive filter instead.

Example

"You are an idiot"

97% toxicity

"I hope you die"

94% toxicity

Abusive

A very hateful, aggressive, disrespectful comment or otherwise very likely to make a user leave a discussion or give up on sharing their perspective. This attribute is much less sensitive to more mild forms of toxicity, such as comments that include positive uses of curse words.

This filter is less sensitive and recommended for general moderation.

Example

"I wish you killed yourself"

89% abusive

"try cutting yourself attention whore"

81% abusive

Identity Attacks

Negative or hateful comments targeting someone because of their identity.

This includes racism, sexism, homophobia, and all general forms of discrimination and/or prejudice.

Example

"Kill all men"

92% identity attack

"gays dont have rights"

90% identity attack

Insults

Insulting, inflammatory, or negative comment towards a person or a group of people.

These insults cover discrimination albeit not to the extent of the insults filter. It may be slightly sensitive so thresholds should be high.

Example

"You're useless"

91% insult

"talking to another guy you slut?"

96% insult

Profanity

Swear words, curse words, or other obscene or profane language.

This filter covers all profane language (including basic swear words including "dumb"). Set the threshold high if these are not desired.

Example

"you're retarded"

78% profanity

"wtf does that mean"

83% profanity

Threats

Describes an intention to inflict pain, injury, or violence against an individual or group.

The threats filter is effective in production, but it is highly recommended to DM appropriate staff of the incident.

Example

"if you don't stfu ill kill you"

97% threats

Sexual

Contains references to sexual acts, body parts, or other lewd content.

This filter can be disabled in NSFW channels.

No examples available

Flirtation

Pickup lines, complimenting appearance, subtle sexual innuendos, etc.

No examples available

Incoherency

Messages which are difficult to understand or nonsensical.

Effective for spam prevention.

Example

"alokwdof"

89% incoherent

Inflammatory

Messages which are intending to provoke or inflame another person or a group of people.

This filter should be used as an addon to the Toxicity & Insults filter.

Example

"You are an idiot"

97% inflammatory

"I hope you die"

94% inflammatory
